2. Scope of this Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Trainara collects, uses, stores, and protects personal data when you use the Trainara mobile application and related services.

By using Trainara, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

3. Personal data we collect

We may collect and process the following categories of personal data:

3.1 Account and identification data

  • Name or alias
  • Email address
  • Preferred language
  • Geographic region or country

3.2 Athletic and performance data

  • Height and weight
  • Training data (e.g. workouts, power, heart rate, cadence)
  • Physical activity history
  • Training preferences and goals

These data are not medical data, but they may relate to physical performance and lifestyle.

3.3 Data from third-party integrations

If you connect external services, we may access data provided by:

  • Strava
  • Intervals.icu
  • Other compatible training platforms

Only data explicitly authorized by you through those services will be processed.

4. How we use your data

Your data are processed for the following purposes:

  • To provide personalized training and nutrition recommendations
  • To adapt recommendations to your goals, fitness level, and availability
  • To improve the quality and relevance of our services
  • To manage your user account
  • To communicate with you regarding the service
  • To ensure technical functionality and security

5. Legal basis for processing

Under the GDPR, Trainara processes your personal data based on:

  • Performance of a contract: providing the services you request
  • Your consent: for optional data and third-party integrations
  • Legitimate interests: improving and securing the service

You may withdraw your consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

6. Artificial intelligence and automated processing

Trainara uses artificial intelligence models to generate recommendations related to training and nutrition.

  • AI-generated outputs are informational and supportive only
  • They are not medical advice and do not replace professional judgment
  • Final decisions on training, nutrition, and health remain your responsibility

Automated processing does not produce legal or similarly significant effects on you.

7. Data sharing and third parties

We do not sell your personal data.

Your data may be shared only with trusted service providers strictly necessary to operate Trainara, including:

  • Cloud infrastructure and hosting providers
  • Artificial intelligence providers (e.g. OpenAI)
  • Integrated training platforms authorized by you

All third parties are subject to appropriate data protection obligations.

8. International data transfers

Some service providers may process data outside the European Economic Area (EEA).

Where applicable, such transfers are safeguarded by appropriate legal mechanisms, such as standard contractual clauses.

9. Data retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the service or until you request deletion, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

10. Your rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Request erasure of your data
  • Restrict or object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw consent at any time
  • Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise your rights, contact us at support@trainara.com.

11. Data security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

However, no system can be guaranteed to be 100% secure.

12. Cookies and tracking

Trainara does not use cookies or third-party tracking technologies for analytics or advertising purposes at this time.

13. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time.

If significant changes are made, we will inform you through the application or by other appropriate means.
